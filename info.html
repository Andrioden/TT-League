News Ladder Matches Info

  • 1. Match Reporting
    • 1.1 All matches should be submitted to André S. Hansen either by mail (andre.ok AT online.no) or irl sound.
    • 1.2 Match submits should contain: Player A name - Player A Score - Player B Score - Player B name - Match Date.
  • 2. Match rules
    • 2.1 We follow the official rules found here.
    • 2.2 A match is over when a player reaches two set points. Meaning 2-0 is a valid end score.
  • 3. Ladder Rounds
    • 3.1 Matches will be played in round events. At these events everyone will play one match each if possible!
    • 3.2 The official Board of Advisors will decide when every round should be played.
    • 3.3 Rounds will be announced in the news section.
    • 3.4 If you fail to meet and play your match during the round event, arrange with your opponent to play it at a later point.
  • 4. Board of Advisors
    • 4.1 Anyone who take part of discussions at the facebook group can consider themselves part of the BoA.
  • 5. Ladder ranking
    • 5.1 The ladder ranking is rated after Points > SetsDiff
    • 5.2 If the top three positions is shared in any way, a Bo7 playoff will determine the final ladder ranking.
    • 5.3 If there are three or more that shares one position a mini group playoff will be done. One match versus each. Bo7.
